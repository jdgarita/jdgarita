# Hi, I'm JD 👋

Google Certified Associate Android Developer in Kotlin with 10+ years of experience building high-quality, maintainable apps for both large enterprises and startups. I specialize in **Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)** and **Compose Multiplatform**, shipping cross-platform apps that run natively on Android and iOS from a shared codebase.

I care about clean architecture, thoughtful developer tooling, and pragmatic engineering — the kind of work that scales with a team and ages well. Comfortable across the full product lifecycle: feature design, testing, debugging, performance tuning, and mentoring.
